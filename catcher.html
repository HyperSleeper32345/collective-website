Catcher

Respond faster. Book more. Waste nothing.

Catcher is Collective's lead capture suite, designed to improve response speed, qualification, booking, and follow-up across the moments where revenue is most often lost.

Book a Strategy Call View the System

Lead capture is where good demand often gets wasted.

Most businesses invest heavily in generating leads but lose a significant portion of that value in the moments after enquiry. Slow response times, inconsistent follow-up, poor qualification, and manual handoffs create a pipeline that leaks revenue at the point where it should be converting.

Catcher is built to close those gaps. It turns lead capture into a structured, measurable process that improves speed, quality, and conversion at every step.

What's included.

Response Workflows

Automated and human-assisted response sequences designed to reach leads within minutes, not hours.

Qualification Logic

Structured qualification criteria that separate high-value opportunities from low-intent enquiries.

Booking Systems

Frictionless scheduling that reduces the steps between enquiry and first conversation.

Enrichment

Lead data enrichment that gives sales teams context before the first call.

Follow-up

Structured follow-up sequences for leads that do not convert on first contact.

What we measure.

Speed-to-First-Touch

Time between enquiry and first meaningful response.

Contact Rate

Percentage of leads successfully reached after enquiry.

Booking Rate

Percentage of contacted leads that schedule a meeting or consultation.

Show Rate

Percentage of booked meetings that actually take place.

Qualified Meeting Rate

Percentage of meetings that meet the criteria for a genuine commercial opportunity.

LQR

Lead qualification rate across the full capture sequence.

A slower handoff creates a weaker pipeline.

Research consistently shows that the probability of qualifying a lead drops significantly with every minute of delay after the initial enquiry. Businesses that respond within five minutes are far more likely to convert than those that take an hour or longer.

Catcher is designed to compress that window and ensure every lead receives a timely, structured response.

When the business already has demand but leaks value after enquiry.

Catcher is the right starting point when leads are coming in but conversion rates are lower than expected. If the business is generating demand through paid channels, referrals, or organic traffic but struggling to turn those enquiries into booked meetings and qualified pipeline, the constraint is likely in the capture layer.

Pitcher

Create demand on purpose.

Pitcher is Collective's lead generation suite, built to turn targeting, messaging, and paid acquisition into qualified pipeline.

Talk to Collective View the System

Lead generation with clearer commercial intent.

Pitcher is designed to generate demand that connects to revenue, not just clicks. Every campaign is built around qualified pipeline creation, with targeting, messaging, and measurement structured to improve the quality of leads entering the system.

That means less wasted spend on vanity metrics and more focus on the signals that actually predict commercial outcomes.

What's included.

Paid Acquisition

Campaign strategy, media buying, and budget allocation across channels that match the buying cycle.

Lead Pages

Conversion-focused landing pages designed for qualification, not just volume.

Tracking and Attribution

Source tracking, conversion attribution, and pipeline connection so every lead has a clear origin.

Testing

Structured testing across messaging, creative, audience, and offer to find what actually moves pipeline.

What we measure.

CTR

Click-through rate as a signal of message relevance and audience alignment.

CVR

Conversion rate from click to enquiry, measuring landing page and offer performance.

CPL

Cost per lead, tracked against qualified opportunity value, not just raw volume.

Qualified Opportunity Flow

The rate at which leads convert into genuine commercial conversations.

Early Pipeline Signals

Indicators that predict downstream revenue quality before the sales cycle completes.

When demand is the real constraint.

Pitcher is the right starting point when the business has a strong offer, a clear market, and the capacity to handle more pipeline, but is not generating enough qualified demand to fill it. If the constraint is not volume but quality, speed, or trust, a different layer may be the better starting point.

Collective always starts by identifying the real constraint before recommending where to begin.
